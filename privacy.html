Privacy Policy

Your data and privacy

ATV Exchange collects user details such as name, email, phone number, payment details, transaction records and KYC information only for account setup, transaction processing, fraud prevention and customer support.

Information We Collect

We may collect profile information, payment method details, transaction history, uploaded verification documents, support messages, device information and notification preferences.

How We Use Information

We use customer information to create accounts, process deposits and withdrawals, confirm transactions, complete verification, prevent fraud, send service updates and respond to support requests.

Data Sharing

Customer data will not be sold. We may share information only when needed to process a transaction, comply with legal requirements, prevent fraud or protect ATV Exchange and its users.

Security

ATV Exchange monitors transactions to prevent fraud, money laundering and unauthorized activity. Suspicious or fraudulent activity may lead to account restriction.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@atvexchange.net.
